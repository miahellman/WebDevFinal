Welcome to Our Outer Space Tour!

Travel from planet to planet and find the hidden star. Each planet has 5 spaceships orbiting around it. Under one, is the star. Select the correct ship and win a point. Finding the star is 1 point, and finding the shooting star is 3 points (appears only the round after finding a star)! Points carry over when game restarts.
